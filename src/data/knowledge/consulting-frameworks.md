Consulting frameworks are mental models that help structure complex problems. This guide covers the most useful ones.

## MECE Principle

MECE (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive) is the foundation of structured thinking. Every problem should be broken down into parts that do not overlap and cover everything.
