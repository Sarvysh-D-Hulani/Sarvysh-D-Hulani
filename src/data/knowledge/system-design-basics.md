System design is often overlooked by AI engineers, but it is critical for deploying models at scale. This guide covers the essentials.

## Why System Design Matters for AI

You can build the best model in the world, but if you cannot serve it efficiently, it does not matter. Understanding system design helps you make better architectural decisions.
