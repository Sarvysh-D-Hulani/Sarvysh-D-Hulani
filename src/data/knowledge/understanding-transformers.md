Transformers have revolutionized AI. This guide breaks down the architecture, explains self-attention, and walks through a practical implementation.

## The Core Idea

The Transformer architecture, introduced in "Attention Is All You Need," replaced recurrent layers with self-attention mechanisms. This allows the model to process all tokens simultaneously rather than sequentially.

## Self-Attention

Self-attention allows each position in the sequence to attend to all other positions. This is what gives Transformers their power — the ability to capture long-range dependencies without recurrence.
