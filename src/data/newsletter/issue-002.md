This week I want to talk about building in public — the practice of sharing your work openly as you build it.

## Why Build in Public?

1. Accountability: When you share publicly, you are more likely to follow through
2. Feedback: You get input from people you would never meet otherwise
3. Network: You attract people who resonate with your work
4. Serendipity: Opportunities find you when your work is visible
